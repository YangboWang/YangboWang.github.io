Biography

I am currently a 3rd-year M.S. Student at the College of Computer Science and Software Engineering, Shenzhen University, supervised by Jie Zhou. My aim is to make deep learning models get rid of the excessive reliance to labeled data. Specifically, my research focuses on Noisy Label Learning, Few-shot Learning, Semi-supervised Learning, Self-supervised Learning and Adversarial Training.
